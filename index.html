Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Pulse CRM. Can't find what you need? Contact support.

How do I invite team members to my workspace?

Go to Settings → Team and click Invite Member. Enter their email address and choose a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). They'll receive an invite link valid for 48 hours.

Can I import contacts from a CSV file?

Yes. Navigate to Contacts → Import, upload your CSV, and map each column to a Pulse CRM field. Duplicate detection runs automatically based on email address.

How do I set up automated email sequences?

Open Automations → Email Sequences, click New Sequence, and define your trigger (e.g., deal stage change, contact tag added). Add steps with delays between each email. Sequences start sending once you activate them.

What integrations are available?

Pulse CRM integrates natively with Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Zapier, HubSpot, and Salesforce. You can also connect any tool via our REST API or webhooks. See the Integrations page for the full list.

How is my data backed up?

Your data is automatically backed up every 6 hours to geographically redundant storage. You can trigger a manual export at any time from Settings → Data → Export. Backups are retained for 90 days.

How do I reset my password?

Click Forgot password on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact support.

Can I customize the pipeline stages?

Yes. Go to Settings → Pipelines, select a pipeline, and drag to reorder stages or click the pencil icon to rename them. You can also add new stages or archive ones you no longer use.

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

Your account enters a 30-day grace period during which you can still export all your data. After 30 days, the account and its data are permanently deleted. We recommend exporting before cancelling.
