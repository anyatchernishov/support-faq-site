Getting Started

Everything you need to know to get started with FlowDeck.

How to invite teammates to a workspace

Go to Settings → Team and click Invite Member. Enter your teammate's email address and select their role — Admin, Editor, or Viewer. They'll receive an invite link by email, valid for 48 hours. Admins can resend invites from the same settings page.

How to archive a completed project board

Open the board, click the ⋯ menu in the top-right corner, and select Archive Board. Archived boards are hidden from your main view but remain fully searchable. You can restore them at any time from Settings → Archived Boards.

Forgot username — how to recover account access

On the FlowDeck login page, click Forgot username? and enter the email address associated with your account. You'll receive an email with your username within a few minutes. If you no longer have access to that email address, contact support@flowdeck.io with proof of account ownership.

How do I set up an automation trigger?

Open your board and click Automations in the top toolbar. Click + Add Automation, then choose a trigger (e.g., "When status changes to Done") and an action (e.g., "Send an email notification"). Review the preview and click Activate. Automations run immediately once active.

Duplicate email notifications after re-enabling automation

This is a known issue: when an automation is re-enabled, any items that already match the trigger condition fire once immediately. To avoid this, temporarily set affected items to a neutral status before re-enabling, then update them after the automation is live.

Slack integration double-posting on status change

Double-posting typically happens when both a Slack integration and an automation are configured for the same trigger. Check Integrations → Slack and Automations to confirm only one is active for that event. Remove or disable the duplicate, then test with a single status change.

CSV import mis-mapping date columns (DD/MM vs MM/DD)

During import, FlowDeck defaults to MM/DD/YYYY. If your CSV uses DD/MM/YYYY, select Custom date format on the column-mapping screen and enter DD/MM/YYYY. You can also pre-convert your dates in Excel or Google Sheets before uploading to avoid the issue.

Trello board import stalling at 80% progress

Large Trello boards (500+ cards) can time out during import. The workaround is to export your Trello board in smaller batches — archive completed cards first, export, then import the active cards. Full large-board import support is on our roadmap for Q3 2025.

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication and click Enable. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or 1Password), enter the 6-digit code to confirm, and save your backup codes somewhere safe. 2FA applies to your account on all devices.

SSO/SAML setup for Enterprise accounts

Enterprise accounts can configure SSO via Settings → Security → Single Sign-On. You'll need your Identity Provider's metadata URL or XML file. Supported IdPs include Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, and OneLogin. After saving the configuration, test with a non-admin account before enforcing SSO for all users.

Request to transfer account ownership to new admin

The current account owner must go to Settings → Account → Ownership Transfer, select the new owner from the member list, and confirm with their password. The new owner will receive a confirmation email. Note: ownership transfer is immediate and cannot be undone without the new owner's cooperation.

Mobile app keeps logging out on Android

This is a known issue affecting Android 13+ devices with aggressive battery optimization enabled. Go to your Android Settings → Apps → FlowDeck → Battery and set it to Unrestricted. If the issue persists, try clearing the app cache. A fix is included in the next mobile release (v4.2.1).

Annual renewal charge — why was I billed now?

FlowDeck subscriptions renew automatically on the same date each year. You can find your renewal date in Settings → Billing → Subscription. A reminder email is sent 14 days before renewal. If you were charged unexpectedly, check that your billing email matches your notification preferences.

Annual vs monthly billing — upgrade charge confusion

When you upgrade from monthly to annual mid-cycle, you're charged the annual rate immediately and your remaining monthly credit is applied as a prorated deduction. The exact amount credited is shown in the upgrade confirmation screen before you confirm.

Refund request within 14-day money-back window

FlowDeck offers a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase or annual renewal. Email support@flowdeck.io with your account email and the charge date. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.
